Information & Data

This storymap is an overview of farmers markets located in Portland, Oregon. Each page features one of the 17 locations including information about hours & days open, accepted currency and contact information. For more information about directions, ventors or interested in working or volunteering for the farmers market please visit the market wesite which are included in the slides.

Geospatial data is credited to The City of Portland, other data about farmers markets was fount at the Portland farmers market website.
